About Us

The Department of Biophysics brings together faculty and students working across traditional research disciplines to gain a quantitative and molecular understanding of biological systems. Monthly research seminars promote collaboration and provide feedback from colleagues with complementary expertise. The department provides a forum for coordinating and strengthening core equipment facilities to bring the latest technical advances to bear on this research. This group is working together to develop integrated curricula to train students working between biology and the physical sciences, computation, and engineering.

The Department of Biophysics brings together a dynamic group of interdisciplinary faculty from both the Morningside and Health Sciences Campuses. Participating faculty represent the Departments of Applied Physics and Applied Mathematics, Biochemistry and Molecular Biophysics, Biological Sciences, Biomedical Engineering, Chemical Engineering, Chemistry, Physics, Mechanical Engineering, Medicine, Microbiology, Ophthalmology, Pharmacology and Physiology and Cellular Biophysics.
